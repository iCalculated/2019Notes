Cayley-Dickson Construction

Idea: Give a multiplication on C2\mathbb{C}^2C2

(a,b)⋅(c,d)=(ac−d‾b,da+bC‾).(a,b)\cdot(c,d)=(ac-\overline{d}b,da+b\overline{C}).(a,b)⋅(c,d)=(ac−db,da+bC).

Allows generation of quaternions from two imaginary numbers.
