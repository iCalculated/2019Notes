Lecture

Overflow

Occurs when when integral types are added and a bit is changed outside of the allocated memory.

Integer addition with fixed bits is a mathematical ring (modulo field).

Underflow

Opposite of overflow, subtracting from a positive number wrapping back around.

C is perfectly fine with doing both of these things, to check automatically would be bad for speed.

Endianness

In what order are bytes processed? If a number starts at #1024 does it continue towards #1025 or #1023?

Big endian starts at the highest order then decreases downwards, machines that could process that format were called ‚Äúbig endian‚Äù machines little endian does the opposite, starting with the least significant bits. There is not a strong objective reason for either side, seems that little is more popular. That is partially Intel‚Äôs fault since they are part of the little endian gang.

Remember this because when looking at lists of bytes they will print in opposite order.

Integer Ops

Algorithms are the same in binary as base ten, but painful due to quantity of digits.

On a computer, the Arithmetic and Logic Unit (ALU) performs the operations. Various ops take different quantities of cycles, where a 1 GHz processor has 1 billion per second. Addition, subtraction, shift, and logical operators take only a single cycle, but multiplication takes 3 and division takes 30. Thus, compilers like to avoid multiplication and division.

  • << is left shift, x<< k means to shift bits of x left by k
