Capacitor

Constructed by two separated conductors. Energy is stored in an electric field between plates.

Capacitance is QV\dfrac{Q}{V}VQ​, measured in terms of farads. However, capacitance is independent of the ratio since the terms are dependent on each other.

In a parallel plate capacitor C=Aϵ0dC=\dfrac{A\epsilon_0}{d}C=dAϵ0​​, dependent only on geometry. While battery is disconnected, pulling plates apart scales voltage and potential energy, but lowers capacitance. Conversely, if the battery is attached then pulling the plates apart lowers capacitance, potential energy, and voltage because charge does not stay on the plates.

Energy density is energy over volume.
